Trees: Nature's Carbon Warriors

Did you know?

A single mature tree can absorb up to 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year!

Why Trees Matter

Carbon Storage

Trees store carbon in their wood, roots, and leaves, helping reduce greenhouse gases.

Clean Air

Trees filter air pollutants and provide fresh oxygen for all living beings.

Ecosystem Support

Trees provide habitat for wildlife and maintain biodiversity.
